Purpose

Programmers often use syntax highlighters when programming. The purpose of this research is to document the effect of such syntax highlighters.

Test Procedure

During the test you will be presented with a series of programming tasks. There will be a snippet of code, and below it a question. Answer the question by writing the answer in the text field below the question. When you think you have the right answer, click "next" to move on. The test takes around 10 minutes.

Withdrawal

Closing the tab or exiting the website at any time before finishing the last task, will cancel the test and your results will not be saved.

Data storage

The results of the test will be stored in a database. No personal information will be stored, only your answers and the time spent.


By clicking "Start test" you herby agree to the above.

NB: The test is timed.
